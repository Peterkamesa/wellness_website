Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about
our services, therapy, and mental well-being.

How do I book a session?

You can book a session directly through our website by visiting the "Practitioners" section and selecting your preferred practitioner. Alternatively, you can call our office to schedule an appointment.

Are online sessions available?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online sessions for your convenience. Many of our practitioners provide telehealth options.

What's the difference between therapy and coaching?

Therapy typically focuses on healing from past trauma and managing mental health conditions, while coaching is more focused on setting and achieving future goals and improving performance.

Can I switch practitioners if I don't feel the right connection?

Absolutely. Finding the right fit is crucial for your healing journey. We encourage you to try different practitioners until you find someone you feel comfortable with.

Do I need a subscription to access services?

No subscription is required. We offer both individual sessions and package options based on your needs.

Is my information and session history kept confidential?

Yes, all client information is kept strictly confidential in accordance with professional ethical guidelines and privacy laws.
