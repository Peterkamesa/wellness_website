NEED HELP?

Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about
our services, therapy, and mental well-being.

What types of therapy do you offer?

We offer a wide range of therapy options including individual therapy, couples counseling, group therapy, and specialized services such as cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based therapy, and trauma-focused therapy.

Can I choose a practitioner based on my preferences?

Yes, you can browse through our list of licensed professionals and choose someone based on their specialization, availability, and approach to therapy that suits your needs.

What should I expect during my first session?

During your first session, your practitioner will get to know you, discuss your goals, and help determine the best path forward. It’s a safe space to share and ask questions about the process.

Do I need a referral to get started?

No referral is needed. You can book a session directly with any of our available practitioners—just choose the one that best fits your needs and schedule.

Are there any free resources available?

Yes, we provide a variety of free resources including blog articles, guided meditations, and self-care tips to support your mental well-being.

How is my privacy protected during sessions?

Your privacy is our top priority. All sessions are conducted securely, and your personal details are protected in line with legal and ethical confidentiality standards.
